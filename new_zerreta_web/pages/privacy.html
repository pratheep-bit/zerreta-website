Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2025

Last Updated: April 14, 2025

Zerreta Learnings ("Zerreta", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • School name, class/grade, and student ID
  • Login credentials

Usage Data

  • Pages visited, time spent, features accessed
  • Assessment and learning progress
  • Feedback and analytics

Device & Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type, OS, and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Personalize learning experiences
  • Analyze performance and improve our platform
  • Communicate updates, offers, or changes
  • Ensure user safety and system security

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data:

  • With educational institutions for academic tracking
  • With trusted third-party partners for hosting and analysis
  • As required by Indian law or to protect user safety

All third parties must adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encrypted storage and secure servers
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Zerreta is built for students, including minors under 18. For children under 13, parental or institutional consent is required to access the platform. We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate supervision.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable)

To make a request, email us at: zerreta.co@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill educational and legal requirements or until requested to be deleted by the user or institution.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.

9. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and relevant Indian privacy laws.
